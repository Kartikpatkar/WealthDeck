
# WealthDeck – Vision & Product Blueprint

> **Tagline:** Your Financial Life. Privately Yours.

## Mission

WealthDeck is a privacy-first, offline-first Progressive Web App (PWA) that helps people understand, organize and grow their financial life without requiring an account, cloud sync, ads, or tracking.

The goal is not to build another expense tracker. The goal is to build a **Personal Finance OS** that users completely own.

## Core Principles

- Privacy First
- Offline First
- User Owns Their Data
- Fast PWA
- No mandatory login
- Import/Export everything
- Beautiful, modern UI
- Local-first intelligence

## Target Users

- Students
- Salaried employees
- Families
- Freelancers
- Small businesses
- Privacy-conscious users
